About Nolwazi

A KZN creative who wears many hats: Digital Strategist, Radio Presenter, MC, Speaker, Facilitator & Content Creator.

I've never been great at picking just one lane, and I've stopped apologising for it. Strategy, content and paid media aren't separate jobs to me. They're one big, colourful ecosystem, and I love living right where they overlap. One morning I'm mapping out the story a brand needs to tell, and that same afternoon I'm deep in a Google Ads dashboard or shaping a post that stops the scroll.

What I actually do

From organic social and SEO to Meta and Google Ads, right up to big, noisy launches, I bring a full toolkit and, just as importantly, the judgement to know which tool the moment is really asking for. It's not about doing everything. It's about doing the right thing well, and making it feel effortless.

On air & on stage

You'll catch me on the Midlands 104FM breakfast show, bringing the same energy to the mic that I bring to a campaign brief. Early starts, proper laughs, and a real love for meeting people where they are. That energy travels: as an MC and speaker I've hosted rooms and graced stages including the prestigious Vtulindlela Summit, reading the crowd and keeping the good vibes moving.

I've worked across metro scale campaigns, personal brands, hospitality, automotive, beauty and entertainment, which is a fancy way of saying I've learned to adapt to any audience, any platform, any room. Different brief, same obsession: work that connects, and work that actually delivers.

Why Work With Me

Most brands don't need more noise. They need someone who understands both the strategy and the story, and who can move between a boardroom, a studio and a stage without missing a beat.

That is exactly where I live. From leading a metro scale launch for Kenako, to going live every weekday on Midlands 104FM, to hosting stages like the Vtulindlela Summit, I bring the same energy whether I'm building a campaign or holding a room.

If you want work that connects with real people and actually moves the numbers, let's make something worth talking about.
